The Why

Why join Reciprocall Health?

Even people who share a common illness have different backgrounds, interests and beliefs. ReciproCall Health fosters meaningful relationships between compatible people who also understand life with a serious illness. Discover new connections just like you for support, mentorship or friendship!

Reciprocall Health

ReciproCall Health leverages a powerful, age-old influence strategy to improve health: Reciprocity.

How it works

Creating meaning relationships

Research has shown that when two compatible peers give and receive social support, their health and quality of life can improve. At ReciproCall Health, we believe that peer support and mentoring works best when people have more in common than a health problem.
